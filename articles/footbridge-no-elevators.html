Pedestrian footbridges waste away with unfinished elevators

27 DECEMBER 2025: In Imphal's pedestrian bridges, elevators were due to be added, with work progressing quickly. Then, it stalled. The elevator project remains incomplete to this day.

Inside, all equipment was put in place except the lift itself. After many years of the probably substandard boards withstanding the elements, they started breaking.

Huge interior damage could be seen inside the structures, after consistent rain exposure. The lack of elevators is proving to be a large inconvenience to the elderly and those with disabilities.

People are now using the areas near elevator doors as a dumping ground, and some of it has gotten into the shaft too.
